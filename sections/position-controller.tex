\section{Position Controller (Task 4)}
\label{sec:position}

\paragraph{Plant.} With Tasks~1+2+3 closed, $G_{pos,\mathrm{outer}}(s) = \mathrm{pos}_\mathrm{ref} \to x$ is 11th-order, 0 RHP poles, RHP zero at $+8.51\,\mathrm{rad/s}$ (inherited), \emph{plus a free integrator at the origin} ($x = \int v\,dt$). Type-1 plant $\Rightarrow$ pure P drives step error to zero -- no I-term needed.

\paragraph{Design.} Unlike Tasks~1--3, $\omega_c$ is iterated against the mission spec (peak $v \geq 0.7\,\mathrm{m/s}$ on a 2\,m move within 10\,s):

\begin{table}[H]
    \centering
    \begin{tabular}{c|c|c|c|c|l}
        $\omega_c$ & $\gamma_M$ & $GM$ & Peak $v$ & Settle (2\,\%) & Verdict \\
        \hline
        $0.2$ & $87^{\circ}$ & $38\,\mathrm{dB}$ & $0.33\,\mathrm{m/s}$ & $20\,\mathrm{s}$ & Fails velocity \& settle \\
        $0.5$ & $66^{\circ}$ & $25\,\mathrm{dB}$ & $0.68\,\mathrm{m/s}$ & $12\,\mathrm{s}$ & Just below $0.7$ \\
        $\mathbf{0.6}$ & $\mathbf{60^{\circ}}$ & $\mathbf{25\,\mathrm{dB}}$ & $\mathbf{0.80}$ & $\mathbf{11\,\mathrm{s}}$ & \textbf{Chosen} \\
    \end{tabular}
    \caption{$\omega_c$ iteration. The 11\,s 2\%-envelope settling is pessimistic; the mission only requires \emph{reaching} 2\,m within 10\,s.}
\end{table}

At $\omega_c = 0.6\,\mathrm{rad/s}$: $\angle G_{pos,\mathrm{outer}}(j0.6) = -122.9^{\circ}$, required Lead $+2.85^{\circ}$ -- tiny. The ideal Lead $(\tau_d s + 1)$ is improper and Simulink's \texttt{Transfer Fcn} block rejects it; the alternative is a proper Lead with a filter pole that costs phase back. Since the required Lead is only $\approx 3^{\circ}$, we drop it. From $|L(j\omega_c)| = 1$: $K_P = 1/1.848 = 0.5411$, $\tau_d = 0$.

\texttt{margin(L\_pos)}: $\omega_c = 0.60$, $\gamma_M \approx 57^{\circ}$ (after Lead drop), $GM = 25.17\,\mathrm{dB}$. Linear 2\,m step: peak $v = 0.753\,\mathrm{m/s}$ ($\geq 0.7$ \checkmark).

\paragraph{Hardware (Test~4).} Mission: \texttt{vel=0, bal=1, log=15 : time=2 ; topos=2, vel=1.2 : time=10}. Final position $1.964\,\mathrm{m}$ ($3.6\,\mathrm{cm}$ short), no overshoot, no late limit cycle. Peak velocity $0.79\,\mathrm{m/s}$ ($\geq 0.7$ \checkmark), peak forward tilt $+17.3^{\circ}$, tilt std $2.93^{\circ}$, peak motor voltage $4.95\,\mathrm{V}$. Cleanest test of the campaign; the linear-model peak-$v$ prediction $0.75\,\mathrm{m/s}$ matches hardware closely.
