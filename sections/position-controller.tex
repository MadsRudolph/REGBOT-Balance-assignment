\section{Position Controller (Task 4)}
\label{sec:position}
\enlargethispage{6\baselineskip}

\paragraph{What's special.} The plant inherits a free integrator from $v \to x$, so the open-loop is \textbf{Type-1}. Pure P drives step error to zero -- no I-term needed. And $\omega_c$ is iterated against the mission spec rather than fixed by cascade rules.

\paragraph{Plant (R1).} Linearise $\mathrm{pos}_{\mathrm{ref}} \to x$ with Tasks~1+2+3 closed: 11th-order $G_{pos,\mathrm{outer}}$, $0$ RHP poles, inherited RHP zero at $+8.51\,\mathrm{rad/s}$, \emph{plus} a free integrator at the origin ($x = \int v\,dt$).

\paragraph{$\omega_c$ iteration.} Target: 2\,m move with peak $v \geq 0.7\,\mathrm{m/s}$ within $10\,\mathrm{s}$.

\paragraph{Design at $\omega_c = 0.6\,\mathrm{rad/s}$.} $\angle G_{pos,\mathrm{outer}}(j0.6) = -122.9^{\circ}$, required Lead $+2.85^{\circ}$. The ideal $(\tau_d s + 1)$ is improper (Simulink rejects it); the proper alternative adds a filter pole that costs $\sim 3^{\circ}$ back. Since the required Lead is only $\approx 3^{\circ}$, we drop it: $\tau_d = 0$, $K_P = \mathbf{0.5411}$. \texttt{margin(L\_pos)}: $\omega_c = 0.60$, $\gamma_M \approx 57^{\circ}$, $GM = 25.17\,\mathrm{dB}$. Linear 2\,m step: peak $v = 0.753\,\mathrm{m/s}$ ($\geq 0.7$ \checkmark).

\paragraph{Hardware (Test~4).} Mission \texttt{vel=0, bal=1, log=15 : time=2 ; topos=2, vel=1.2 : time=10}. Final $\mathbf{1.964\,m}$ ($3.6\,\mathrm{cm}$ short), no overshoot. Peak $v\!=\!0.79\,\mathrm{m/s}$ ($\geq 0.7$ \checkmark), peak tilt $+17.3^{\circ}$, $V_{\max}\!=\!4.95\,\mathrm{V}$. The linear $0.75\,\mathrm{m/s}$ peak-$v$ prediction matches hardware.
