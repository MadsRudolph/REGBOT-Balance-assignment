\section{Velocity Controller (Task 3)}
\label{sec:velocity}

\paragraph{Plant.} Linearise $\theta_\mathrm{ref} \to v$ with Tasks~1+2 closed: 9th-order $G_{vel,\mathrm{outer}}$, 0 RHP poles (balance loop did its job), one RHP zero at $+8.51\,\mathrm{rad/s}$ (non-minimum phase: wheels must roll backward to tilt forward), and a free integrator inherited from Task~2's post-integrator. The RHP zero caps achievable bandwidth via the rule-of-thumb $\omega_c \leq z/5 \approx 1.7\,\mathrm{rad/s}$.

\paragraph{Design.} Specs $\omega_c = 1\,\mathrm{rad/s}$ (well under the RHP-zero ceiling and 15$\times$ slower than Task~2), $\gamma_M \geq 60^{\circ}$, $N_i = 3$. PI zero at $\tau_i = N_i/\omega_c = 3.000\,\mathrm{s}$. Phase balance at $\omega_c$: $\angle G_{vel,\mathrm{outer}}(j1) = -92.6^{\circ}$, $\phi_{PI} = -18.4^{\circ}$, required Lead $-9.0^{\circ}$ -- negative, so no Lead. From $|L(j\omega_c)| = 1$:
\begin{equation}
    K_P = \frac{1}{|C_{PI}(j1)\,G_{vel,\mathrm{outer}}(j1)|} = \frac{1}{6.325} = 0.1581.
\end{equation}
Final controller: $C_{vel}(s) = 0.1581 \cdot (3s + 1)/(3s)$. \texttt{margin(L\_vel)}: $\omega_c = 1.00\,\mathrm{rad/s}$, $\gamma_M = 68.98^{\circ}$, $GM = 5.84\,\mathrm{dB}$, 0 RHP closed-loop poles.

\paragraph{Hardware (Test~3b, $0.8\,\mathrm{m/s}$ square).} Mission (repeated 4$\times$): \texttt{vel=0.8 : dist=1 ; vel=0.8, tr=0.2 : turn=90}. Robot completed all four sides and three turns without falling. Peak tilt $+25.5^{\circ}$ (corner acceleration), tilt std $5.03^{\circ}$, peak motor voltage $7.31\,\mathrm{V}$ ($91\,\%$ of the $\pm 8\,\mathrm{V}$ budget -- cost of the higher inner-loop gain on sharp corner $v_\mathrm{ref}$ steps). Cumulative heading error $0.06\,\%$.
