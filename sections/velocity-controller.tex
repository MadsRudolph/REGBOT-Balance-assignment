\section{Velocity Controller (Task 3)}
\label{sec:velocity}

\paragraph{What's special.} An RHP zero at $+8.51\,\mathrm{rad/s}$ caps the achievable bandwidth: rule-of-thumb $\omega_c \leq z/5 \approx 1.7\,\mathrm{rad/s}$. Otherwise the standard recipe (§\ref{sec:architecture}) applies directly.

\paragraph{Plant (R1).} Linearise $\theta_{\mathrm{ref}} \to v$ with Tasks~1+2 closed: 9th-order $G_{vel,\mathrm{outer}}$, $0$ RHP poles (the balance loop took care of those), one RHP zero at $+8.51\,\mathrm{rad/s}$ (non-minimum phase -- wheels roll backward to tilt forward), plus a free integrator inherited from Task~2's post-integrator.

\paragraph{Recipe application (R2--R5).}

\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{rl}
$\omega_c,\ \gamma_M,\ N_i$ & $1\,\mathrm{rad/s}$ (under RHP-zero cap of $1.7$, and $15\times$ slower than Task~2),\ $60^{\circ}$,\ $3$ \\
PI zero: $\tau_i = N_i / \omega_c$ & $3.000\,\mathrm{s}$ \\
$\angle G_{vel,\mathrm{outer}}(j1)$ & $-92.6^{\circ}$ \\
$\phi_{PI}$ & $-18.4^{\circ}$ \\
Required $\phi_{\mathrm{Lead}}$ & $-9.0^{\circ}$ (negative) -- no Lead \\
$K_P = 1 / 6.325$ & $\mathbf{0.1581}$ \\
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\noindent Final controller: $C_{vel}(s) = 0.1581 \cdot (3s + 1)/(3s)$.

\paragraph{Verify (R6).} \texttt{margin(L\_vel)}: $\omega_c = 1.00\,\mathrm{rad/s}$, $\gamma_M = 68.98^{\circ}$, $GM = 5.84\,\mathrm{dB}$, $0$ RHP closed-loop poles.
