\section{Wheel Speed Controller (Task 1)}
\label{sec:wheel-speed}

\paragraph{Why it is simple.} The plant is first order, so this is the cleanest run of the shared recipe (§\ref{sec:architecture}) and no Lead is needed.

\paragraph{Plant (R1).} The Day~5 on-floor test gives a single-pole plant:
\[
G_{vel}(s) = \frac{2.198}{s + 5.985}, \qquad
\text{DC gain } 0.367\,\mathrm{(m/s)/V}, \qquad
\tau = 167\,\mathrm{ms}.
\]

\paragraph{Recipe application (R2--R5).}

\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{rl}
$\omega_c,\ \gamma_M,\ N_i$ & $30\,\mathrm{rad/s},\ 60^{\circ},\ 3$ \\
PI zero: $\tau_i = N_i / \omega_c$ & $0.100\,\mathrm{s}$ \\
$\angle G_{vel}(j30)$ & $-78.7^{\circ}$ \\
$\phi_{PI} = \arctan(N_i) - 90^{\circ}$ & $-18.4^{\circ}$ \\
Combined phase, natural $\gamma_M$ & $-97.1^{\circ}$, \ $82.9^{\circ}$ -- well above spec, no Lead \\
$|L|_{\mathrm{unscaled}}$ at $\omega_c$ & $0.0758$ \\
$K_P = 1 / |L|_{\mathrm{unscaled}}$ & $\mathbf{13.20}$ \\
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\noindent Final controller: $C_{wv}(s) = 13.20 \cdot (0.1s + 1)/(0.1s)$.

\paragraph{Verification (R6).} \texttt{margin(L\_wv)}: $\omega_c = 30.00\,\mathrm{rad/s}$, $\gamma_M = 82.85^{\circ}$, $GM = \infty$ (with only two open-loop poles, the phase asymptotes at $-180^{\circ}$ but never crosses it, so no finite gain destabilises the loop).

\paragraph{Hardware (Test~0).} Mission \texttt{bal=0, vel=0.3, log=15 : time=3} (robot on its side, balance disabled). It rose to $0.27\,\mathrm{m/s}$ in $\mathbf{0.087\,s}$, with peak motor voltage $2.60\,\mathrm{V}$ and the L/R wheels agreeing to within $0.76\,\%$. The first-order prediction $t_r \approx 2.2/\omega_c \approx 73\,\mathrm{ms}$ matches within one $15\,\mathrm{ms}$ log sample.
