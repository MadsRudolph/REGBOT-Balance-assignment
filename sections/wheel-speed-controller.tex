\section{Wheel Speed Controller (Task 1)}
\label{sec:wheel-speed}

\paragraph{Plant.} Day~5 on-floor 1-pole identification: $G_{vel}(s) = 2.198/(s + 5.985)$ (DC gain $0.367\,\mathrm{(m/s)/V}$, $\tau = 167\,\mathrm{ms}$).

\paragraph{Design.} Specs $\omega_c = 30\,\mathrm{rad/s}$, $\gamma_M \geq 60^{\circ}$, $N_i = 3$. PI form: $C_{wv}(s) = K_p (\tau_i s + 1)/(\tau_i s)$ with $\tau_i = N_i/\omega_c = 0.100\,\mathrm{s}$. Phase balance at $\omega_c$: $\angle G_{vel}(j30) = -78.7^{\circ}$, $\phi_{PI} = -18.4^{\circ}$, total $-97.1^{\circ}$ giving natural $\gamma_M = 82.9^{\circ}$ -- comfortably above spec, no Lead. From $|L(j\omega_c)| = 1$:
\begin{equation}
    K_p = \frac{1}{|C_{PI}(j\omega_c)\,G_{vel}(j\omega_c)|} = \frac{1}{0.0758} = 13.2037.
\end{equation}
Final controller: $C_{wv}(s) = 13.2037 \cdot (0.1s + 1)/(0.1s)$. \texttt{margin(L\_wv)}: $\omega_c = 30.00\,\mathrm{rad/s}$, $\gamma_M = 82.85^{\circ}$, $GM = \infty$.

\paragraph{Hardware (Test~0).} Mission: \texttt{bal=0, vel=0.3, log=15 : time=3}. Robot laid on its side, balance disabled. Rise to $0.27\,\mathrm{m/s}$ in $0.012\,\mathrm{s}$, peak motor voltage $2.60\,\mathrm{V}$, L/R wheel agreement $0.76\,\%$. Hardware rise time matches the designed first-order-equivalent prediction $\tau_{\mathrm{cl}} \approx 2.2/\omega_c \approx 73\,\mathrm{ms}$ (within one $15\,\mathrm{ms}$ log sample).
